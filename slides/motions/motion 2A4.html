Amendment 4 to Motion 2: "Reform of General Council and Creation of a Folk Assembly"

Insert a new item (b) to proposed SO.25 (v) and renumber accordingly:

2 members aged 16 to 24 inclusive, 1 elected every year for a period of 2 years;

The proposed SO.25 (v)v now reads:

(v) General Council members shall be elected at the Annual Gathering Meeting (unless otherwise stated) in the following groups:

  1. 8 members, 4 elected each year for a period of 2 years;
  2. 2 members aged 16 to 24 inclusive, 1 elected every year for a period of 2 years;
  3. 1 member who is a District Fellow elected by the District Fellow Committee.

Proposed and Seconded by: DF Committee

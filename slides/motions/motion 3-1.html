Motion 3. System for Elections to General Council

This Annual General Meeting believes the present Single Transferable Vote (STV) election system is not fit for purpose and should be replaced by a block vote system from 2014 onwards.

This Annual General Meeting resolves to change the Standing Order for General Meetings and Elections of Woodcraft Folk document by replacing clause SO.25(i) with the following:

  1. Elections shall be conducted by a block vote system, as defined by the Electoral Reform Society. SOC shall publish the break-down of all results after any election which is conducted.

Proposed and seconded by: Ealing and Hammersmith District

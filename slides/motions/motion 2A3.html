Amendment 3 to Motion 2: "Reform of General Council and Creation of a Folk Assembly"

Replace proposed GC.1 (ii):

Joint Centres and Campsites Committee, to run the work of its centres and campsites;

With:

Joint Centres and Campsites Committee, to work with and support the management committees of the individual centres;

Replace SO.25 (ix).b:

1 member selected by the General Council from the movement to represent each Centre or Campsite management committee.

With

1 member from the movement selected by each centre or campsite management committee to represent them.

Proposed and Seconded by: Sheffield Porter and Don district

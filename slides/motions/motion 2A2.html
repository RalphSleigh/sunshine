Amendment 2 to Motion 2: "Reform of General Council and Creation of a Folk Assembly"

Append "plus one representative elected by each Region or Nations annual conference." to proposed SO.25 (vii) so that it shall read:

  1. Folk Assembly shall be made up of all members of General Council, plus the elected members of Campaigns and Communications Committee, Education and Groups Committee and the Joint Centres and Campsites Committee, plus one representative elected by each Region or Nations annual conference.

Proposed and Seconded by: Gwerin y Coed (Woodcraft Folk in Wales)

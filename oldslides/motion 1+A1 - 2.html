Motion 1. Timetable for Annual Gathering + Amendment 1

This Annual General Meeting agrees to make the following changes to the standing orders:

  1. Replace SO.8 with the following text:

    SO.8 The Standing Orders Committee shall:

    1. Ensure that there are sufficient delegates present to form a quorum:
      1. For the Annual General Meeting to begin, one hundred delegates, or representatives of one fifth of the total number of registered groups is required, whichever is lower.
      2. For further sessions of the Annual General Meeting, a majority of the delegates attending the General Meeting is required.
    2. Make a report to the Annual General Meeting formally presenting the Final Agenda and the procedure for the conduct of elections.

    THIS REMOVES THE REFERENCE TO ENSURING NO DELEGATE HOLDS MORE THAN ONE VOTING CARD

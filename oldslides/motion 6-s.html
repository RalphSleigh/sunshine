Motion 6. Individual & Group Membership Fees

Supporting Information:

At the time of writing we have 322 paying groups, 1,348 waged adult members, 1,106 unwaged or low waged adult members and 4,186 young members. We changed the membership fee in 2012 but this has led to a shortfall in fees to the core budget.

ModelGroups (322) Group members (4,186) Waged members (1,348)Unwaged or low waged members (1,106) Total income for core budget
Pre 2012 £40,250 N/A £33,700 £11,060 £85,010
2012 £16,100 £20,930 £33,700 £11,060 £81,790
Proposed model £11,270 £79,680 £90,950

It may be that groups take ownership of all individual membership fees and that adult members pay subs. But having a system where the fee is the same is a much fairer system and we encourage those who can afford more donate more.
